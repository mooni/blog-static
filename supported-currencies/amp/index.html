What currencies can be used in Mooni ?

Mooni - 10 Mar 2021

Mooni currently supports cashing out from the Ethereum blockchain.

We are in the process of supporting more chains, L2s and rollups, such as Polygon (Matic), xDAI, Binance Smart Chain, RSK and Polkadot's parachains.

The easiest currency to cash out is the Ether (ETH)
